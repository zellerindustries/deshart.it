About

Hi! I'm Fabio Lione, also known as Desh_Art. I’m a freelance illustrator specializing in Character Design and Fantasy Illustration. I help role-players, writers, and content creators bring their ideas to life through visually compelling, memorable characters.

My work emphasizes anatomy, storytelling, and atmosphere, ensuring each character conveys personality, history, and emotion. I strive to create illustrations that are both striking and narratively rich, making your characters feel alive on the page.
